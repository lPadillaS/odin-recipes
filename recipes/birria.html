Birria

ingredients:

  • 1kg tomatos
  • 5 dry ancho chillies
  • 8 dry guajillo chillies
  • 1 onion
  • 8 (or more) garlic cloves
  • 2 kg beef ribs in chunks (or more)
  • Whatever spices you'd like!

Birria is a mexican meat and dried chilli stew, it can be made with goat, beef, lamb, mutton, pork, or whatever you'd prefer!
